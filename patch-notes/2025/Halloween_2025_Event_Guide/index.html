    Halloween Event 2025: "Spooky Season of the Dead"¶

    Player Guide¶

    Quick Start¶

    Event Duration: Halloween Season 2025 Recommended Level: 50+ (Main Quest), 70-90 (Normal Dailies), 95+ (Hard Dailies) Location: Niflheim

    Getting There: - Talk to Lude NPCs in major cities (Prontera, Geffen, Payon, Morocc, Alberta, Aldebaran) for FREE warp to Niflheim

    Main Quest: "Investigation of the Dead"¶

    Overview¶

    • Start NPC: Grave-Herald in Niflheim (288,269)
    • Level Requirement: 50+
    • Restriction: ONE character per account only
    • Unlocks: Daily Quests (account-wide access after completion)

    Quest Steps¶

    Part 1: Field Hunt Trial¶

    1. Talk to Grave-Herald in Niflheim to accept quest
    2. Go to nif_fild01 (Grave-Herald can warp you)
    3. Talk to Apprentice Grave-Keeper (234,249)
    4. Hunt across nif_fild01 and nif_fild02:
    5. 60x Garling
    6. 20x Skelion (10 on each field)
    7. Return to Apprentice Grave-Keeper

    Rewards: - 35,000 Base EXP - 17,500 Job EXP - 20 Halloween Coins

    Part 2: Dungeon Hunt¶

    1. Enter Niflheim Dungeon F1 (nif_dun01)
    2. Hunt the following monsters:
    3. 30x Ghost Cube
    4. 20x Gan Ceann
    5. 15x Brutal Murderer
    6. Return to Grave-Herald in Niflheim

    Rewards: - 70,000 Base EXP - 35,000 Job EXP - 70 Halloween Coins - 35 Deviling Coins

    Important: Completing the main quest unlocks Daily Quests for ALL characters on your account!

    Daily Quests¶

    Overview¶

    • NPC: Event Wizard at nif_fild01 (233,227)
    • Requirement: Main quest completed (account-wide)
    • Limit: 4 quests per day
    • Reset: Daily at midnight
    • Restriction: Only ONE active quest at a time per account

    How It Works¶

    1. Talk to Event Wizard and select "Take Daily Quest"
    2. Choose difficulty: Normal or Hard
    3. Hunt 30 of the assigned monster
    4. Return and select "Claim Rewards" to get your rewards

    Normal Mode (Level 70-90)¶

    Locations: Niflheim Fields (nif_fild01/02) or Dungeon F1 Monster Pool: - Skelion - Garling - Ghost Cube - Lude Gal - Gan Ceann - Brutal Murderer

    Rewards per quest: - 35-49 Halloween Coins - 15-22 Deviling Coins - 12-19 Black Soul

    Hard Mode (Level 95+)¶

    Location: Niflheim Dungeon F2 ONLY Warning: Extremely dangerous! Party recommended! Monster Pool: - Disguiser - Blue Moon Loli Ruri - Grote - Pierrotzoist

    Rewards per quest: - 50-70 Halloween Coins - 25-35 Deviling Coins - 18-28 Black Soul

    Tip: Complete all 4 daily quests for maximum rewards!

    Side Quest: "Scatelon's Secret"¶

    Overview¶

    • NPC: Old Sage of Bones in Alberta (218,30)
    • Requirement: Main quest completed
    • Restriction: ONE Scatelon Crate per account (this is the rarest pet!)

    Requirements¶

    This is a MAJOR undertaking! You need: - 120x Skelion Bone Fragment (from Skelion) - 300x Halloween Coins - 100x Deviling Coins - 250x Black Soul (from Ghost Cube, Disguiser, Pierrotzoist) - 25x Captured Soul (from Skelion) - 200x Pumpkin Cake (from various Halloween mobs) - 500,000 Zeny

    Reward¶

    • Scatelon Crate - Contains the legendary Scatelon Pet!

    Note: This is designed as a long-term goal spanning both fields and dungeons. The Scatelon pet is worth it!

    Event Currency & Materials Guide¶

    Primary Currencies¶

    Halloween Coin - Main event currency - Drops from all Halloween monsters - Used for costume crafting and Scatelon quest


    Deviling Coin - Premium currency - Drops from harder monsters (F1 mid/high tier, F2 all) - Required for all costume crafting - Used for Gacha spins


    Black Soul - Essential crafting material - Best sources: Ghost Cube (F1), Disguiser & Pierrotzoist (F2) - Required for most costumes - Needed for Scatelon quest (250 required!)


    Captured Soul - Rare material - Exclusive drop from Skelion only - Required for premium/high-tier costumes - Needed for Scatelon quest (25 required)


    Crafting Materials¶

    Skelion Bone Fragment - Exclusive drop from Skelion - Main quest + Scatelon side quest material (120 needed!) - Best farmed in fields


    Other Materials: - Pumpkin Cake - From various Halloween mobs (200 for Scatelon) - Cookie Bat - From Garling, Jakk (budget costumes) - Pumpkin Decor - From Garling (mid-tier costumes) - White Thin Stem - From Garling (specific costumes) - Transform Candy (Red/Blue/Yellow/Green) - From F2 mobs (premium costumes)

    Tips & Strategies¶

    For Beginners (Level 50-70)¶

    1. Start with the Main Quest to unlock dailies
    2. Farm Niflheim Fields for Skelion and Garling
    3. Focus on Normal Mode daily quests
    4. Save coins for budget-tier costumes (65-95 coins)

    For Mid-Level (Level 70-90)¶

    1. Farm Niflheim Dungeon F1 for better spawn density
    2. Complete all 4 Normal Mode dailies each day
    3. Start collecting materials for medium-tier costumes
    4. Work towards Scatelon side quest materials

    For High-Level (Level 95+)¶

    1. Party up for Dungeon F2 Hard Mode dailies
    2. Hard Mode gives significantly better rewards
    3. Farm F2 for Black Soul efficiently
    4. Aim for premium-tier costumes

    Farming Efficiency¶

    • For Halloween Coins: Do all 4 daily quests (140-196 coins/day Normal, 200-280 coins/day Hard)
    • For Skelion materials: Focus on fields (nif_fild01/02)
    • For Black Soul: Farm Ghost Cube (F1) or Disguiser/Pierrotzoist (F2)
    • For Pumpkin Decor/Cookie Bat: Farm Garling in fields or F1

    Account Planning¶

    • Main Quest: Choose your strongest character (account restriction)
    • Daily Quests: Can be done on any character after main quest completion
    • Side Quest: Plan carefully - only ONE Scatelon Crate per account ever!

    Frequently Asked Questions¶

    Q: Can I do the main quest on multiple characters? A: No, only ONE character per account can complete the main quest. Choose wisely!

    Q: Can all my characters do daily quests? A: Yes! Once you complete the main quest on one character, ALL characters on your account can do dailies. However, only one character can have an active daily quest at a time.

    Q: How many Scatelon pets can I get? A: Only ONE per account. This is the rarest pet in the event!

    Q: What's the fastest way to get Halloween Coins? A: Complete all 4 daily quests every day. Hard Mode gives more coins but requires level 95+ and is very difficult.

    Q: Should I save coins or buy costumes now? A: Depends on your goals! If you want the Scatelon pet, save 300 coins for the side quest. Otherwise, costumes are permanent rewards.

    Q: Is F2 soloable? A: The monsters are level 108-115 and very strong. While possible for some classes, it's strongly recommended to bring a party!

    Q: Where do I get Pumpkin Decor? A: Farm Garling monsters in the fields or dungeon F1.

    Q: Can I abandon and retake daily quests? A: Yes, but all progress is lost. You can only complete 4 total quests per day.

!!! info "Food Buff Status Dynamics"
    - Using `+10` Str food and adding a `+7` Str food afterwards:
        - Both buffs will be applied, but only the effect of the `+10` Str will be in effect.
        - When the `+10` Str wears off, the `+7` food will immediately take effect.
    - Using a higher tier food than currently applied will end the lower tier and start
      at its full duration.

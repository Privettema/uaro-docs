## 🎮 Gameplay

### Pet Skin Swap

Pet skin swap implementation.

!!! info "How It Works"
    - Requires a fully loyal pet
    - With the pet out, talk to the **Pet Stylist** NPC in **Prontera (`218, 224`)**, just north of the
      Pet Groomer, to exchange your loyal pet in for a permanent skin swap *(you will lose the pet egg
      itself and will not be turned back into egg form at any time)*
    - This skin is now permanently available **account wide** to swap for any pet
    - This will persist through logout
    - Returning a pet to egg form regardless of skin prior will change back to default when rehatching a pet

### @rodexlog Command

### #Channel System Rework

Reworked the `#channel` system — it now lives within **Settings** with its own configuration.

!!! info "What Changed"
    - Now within settings with its own configuration
    - Private channels now properly persist *(will auto-delete if there is no chat activity for `30 days`, or if the channel owner doesn't login for `30 days`)*
    - Channel owner leaving the channel destroys the private channel
    - Leaving or being kicked will add a `30 minute` delay timer to rejoin
    - Private channels are now invite only

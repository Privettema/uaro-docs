Global Updates
- Roulette System is Live!
	- Cost: 1 Roulette Gold Coin or 10 Roulette Silver Coin(Starts at row 3)
	- Mechanics:
		- Coins are etc items and must be present at time of opening Roulette interface.
		- Rolls prioritize 10 silver coins then 1 Gold coin if 10 requirment not available.
		- If you land on a silver coin you "Fail" and will collect the silver coin and have to start again at the beginning of your currency on hand.
		- Landing on anything but silver coin will allow you to "claim" that item or try your luck on the next tier.
		- Top row claim will be the only option you land on.
		- Odds are based on the number of items within the row and all hold the same chance as any other. Example: 7 items within the row, divide number of items you hope you land on divided by 7.  3/7 = 42.85% chance to get something you want.
	- Bonus:
		- At the start of your roll, you have a 1% chance to trigger a Bonus item to land on within the top row.  If you land on that specific item within only your current roll, you will also receive a 1000 Poring coin reward in conjunction with the claimed item.
	Good Luck!

- Eternal Bastion: 100 Waves of Chaos! Adventurers, the ultimate challenge awaits! The Eternal Bastion is here, featuring 100 waves of escalating enemies. Your mission is simple: clear all 100 waves before the timer runs out. No gimmicks, no distractions—just pure combat and teamwork. Can your party survive the onslaught?
	- 1. Wave Progression:
		- Wave 1-20: Beginner mobs (e.g., Porings, Fabres, Lunatics). A warm-up phase to ease into the challenge.
		- Wave 21-40: Intermediate mobs  Enemies start to hit harder.
		- Wave 41-60: Advanced mobs  Expect tougher enemies.
		- Wave 61-80: Elite mobs  Monsters with high HP, strong AoE attacks, and status effects(New status every floor START FROM THIS POINT that pops on the map itself every 20-30 seconds).
		- Wave 81-99: Bio4 / HTF/ OGH monsters These waves will push your party to its limits with high-damage mobs and complex mechanics.
		- Wave 100: Final MVP Boss. A unique MVP with devastating skills and mechanics, changing every month to keep the challenge fresh.
	- 2. Objective:
		- Clear all 100 waves of mobs and the final MVP within 4 hours.
		- No defense mechanics—just focus on defeating the waves as quickly and efficiently as possible.
	- 3. Failure Condition:
		- The instance fails if the 4-hour timer runs out before all waves are cleared or all party memebers die.
	- 4. Requirements and restrictions
		- 12 person minimum party.
		- 1 week account bound cooldown.
		- No loot from mobs.
